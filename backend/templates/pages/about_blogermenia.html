About Blogermenia

A content platform powered by a reusable backend framework.

Blogermenia combines a Next.js frontend with a FastAPI backend. The backend is powered by Backbone, a reusable internal framework designed to help developers ship APIs, admin tools, auth flows, media handling, and template-driven pages with less repeated code.
